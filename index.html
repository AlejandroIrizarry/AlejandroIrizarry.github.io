Background

Hello! I’m Alejandro, a 21-year old Puerto Rican undergraduate student at the University of Puerto Rico at Bayamon. Currently, I’m on my 4th year of my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science learning about important topics such as Data Structures & Algorithms, Web Programming, Computer Architecture, Commercial Programming, among others concepts that prepare me to be a software engineer. To this day, I continue to participate in clubs and associations that empower me to learn new things using resources that I wouldn’t normally find in a classrooms, such as coding competitions and tech workshops.

Right now, I continue to pursue leadership roles on my institution’s Honor Studies Program as President, where I help other students partake on research at our university. I’m also a member of the Association of Computing Machinery and the Computer Science Student Association, which is a great student club since it enables me to network with other people that share my interests and goals.

In addition to my courses, I have been able do apply my knowledge and programming skills in hands-on settings in separated programs.
